### **Ques 1: Learn and Write Definition of Proper Noun**

A **Proper Noun** is the **name of a specific person, place, or thing**.  
It **always starts with a capital letter**.

**Examples**:  
- **Ravi** is my friend.  
- I live in **Delhi**.  
- We went to **Taj Mahal**.
